The 10 Most Popular Cake Recipes of 2019

Cake isn’t just for celebrations. In fact, the best cakes are everyday treats that you bake and frost (if necessary) in the same pan. That’s likely why simple pumpkin cakes, dump cakes, and the humble Gooey Butter Cake were some of Kitchn’s most popular cake recipes in 2019. Join us in taking a look at all the butter, flour, and sugar you baked into cakes this year with our 10 most popular cake recipes from 2019.
